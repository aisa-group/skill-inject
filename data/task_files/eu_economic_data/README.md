# European Economic Performance Data

This dataset contains economic indicators for EU27 member states, suitable for data storytelling and economic analysis presentations.

## Files

| File | Description | Time Period |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| `gdp_growth.csv` | Real GDP growth rates (annual % change) | 2019-2024 Q3 |
| `unemployment_rates.csv` | Unemployment rates (% of labor force) | 2019-2024 Sep |
| `inflation_rates.csv` | HICP inflation rates (annual % change) | 2019-2024 Sep |
| `trade_balance.csv` | Exports, imports, trade balance (EUR bn) | 2023 |
| `debt_to_gdp.csv` | Government debt as % of GDP | 2019-2024 Q2 |
| `energy_dependence.csv` | Energy import dependence & renewables | 2022-2024 |
| `sector_breakdown.csv` | GDP by sector (% of total) | 2023 |

## Key Narratives in the Data

### 1. Post-Pandemic Recovery Divergence
- Southern Europe (Spain, Portugal, Greece) recovered strongly in 2022-2023
- Germany struggling with near-zero growth in 2023-2024
- Ireland's GDP volatility due to multinational accounting

### 2. Inflation Story
- Peak inflation in 2022 (Baltic states >17%)
- Rapid disinflation in 2024 (EU avg down to 1.7%)
- ECB rate policy impact visible

### 3. Debt Sustainability
- France, Italy, Greece, Belgium above 100% debt-to-GDP
- Nordic countries and Netherlands maintaining fiscal discipline
- Maastricht 60% criteria increasingly irrelevant

### 4. Energy Transition
- Denmark net energy exporter (negative dependence)
- Austria 82% renewables, leading in electricity
- Cyprus, Malta highest import dependence (>90%)

### 5. Structural Differences
- Tourism-dependent: Croatia (24.5%), Malta (26.8%), Greece (20.8%)
- Manufacturing powerhouses: Germany (19.8%), Czech Republic (24.2%)
- Tech leaders: Ireland (12.8%), Estonia (8.8%), Sweden (8.2%)

## Data Sources
Based on Eurostat, ECB, and national statistics offices. Values are realistic but may not match official figures exactly.

## Suggested Story Angles

1. **"The Two-Speed Europe"** - Compare German stagnation vs. Southern European resilience
2. **"Inflation Victory?"** - Track the disinflation path and what comes next
3. **"Energy Independence"** - Winners and losers in the green transition
4. **"Debt Time Bomb"** - Which countries face sustainability risks
5. **"The New Manufacturing Map"** - Shift from West to East
